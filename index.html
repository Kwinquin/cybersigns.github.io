Whether it be billboards or posters, signs and physical advertisement have been around for centuries. It was the easiest way companies could show off their products to the most amount of people around a block, or maybe a city, or maybe even the entire country. Throughout the decades, companies adapted to constantly changing technology. Their advertising became mobile, bigger, and more intrusive. Decades further into a hypothetical future—in a world where unregulated capitalism reigns supreme—these signs continue to populate the landscape of cyberpunk, giving the viewer a sense of resemblance in an uncanny world.
